Towards Smarter Decisions on Earth

Suha is an AI-powered tool that transforms satellite imagery into precise textual descriptions, helping both experts and non-experts understand data and make informed decisions.

Why Suha?

"Suha is a bright star in the Al-Na'ama constellation, sometimes referred to as the 'Suha Moon.' It was used as a guide for people during nighttime travels. The Suha platform draws inspiration from this guiding role, aiming to transform satellite imagery into understandable information — serving as a beacon for researchers and space explorers to make accurate and informed decisions on Earth."

Mission

Providing an intelligent tool for fast and precise satellite image analysis, enabling informed decision-making in critical fields.
