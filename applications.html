Applications

Intelligent Vision to Enhance Vital Sectors

Energy

By analyzing satellite images, Suha helps monitor energy networks, such as pipelines, to detect leaks or failures early. This improves energy management and boosts infrastructure sustainability.

Security & Defense

We leverage smart satellite image analysis to monitor sensitive areas, like borders and strategic facilities, enhancing the ability of security agencies to respond swiftly to threats or environmental changes.

Agriculture

Through satellite image monitoring, Suha tracks crop growth and detects environmental changes like water shortages or pest infestations. This empowers farmers to make informed decisions for sustainable production

Environment

By analyzing space imagery, Suha contributes to tracking environmental changes, such as deforestation or pollution, supporting effective actions to protect ecosystems and natural resources
